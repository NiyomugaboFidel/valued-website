Our Programs

Modern Dance Program

This is where passion meets movement! Urban Moves invites kids to express themselves with freedom and energy through contemporary dance styles. From hip-hop beats to freestyle creativity, children learn rhythm, choreography, and teamwork while having a blast. From learning modern instruments to performing in live showcases, students experience a journey of joy, discipline, and deep modern pride.

Traditional Dance Program

Step into the rhythm of Rwanda! Our Traditional Dance program connects children to their cultural roots through joyful movement, drumming, and storytelling. As they learn dances passed down through generations, children gain confidence, coordination, and a deep sense of pride in their heritage. From learning cultural instruments to performing in live showcases, students experience a journey of joy, discipline, and deep cultural pride.

Football Skills Program

Goal! Our football program is more than sport — it’s a training ground for discipline, teamwork, leadership, and fun! Kids learn techniques, teamwork, fair play, and resilience while building fitness and forming lasting friendships. On the field, every child is a champion, encouraged to grow in confidence and skill. From learning modern instruments to performing in live showcases, students experience a journey of joy, discipline, and deep football pride.

Arts & Crafts Program

Color, creativity, and imagination come alive in this joyful art adventure! In our Creative Hands program, children explore painting, drawing, crafting, and building with different materials. Every session is a chance to create something new and exciting. More than fun, art teaches patience, problem-solving, and self-expression. From learning modern instruments to performing in live showcases, students experience a journey of joy, discipline, and deep arts pride.

Public Speaking & Debate

Unlock the power of words! Our language program transforms learning into an exciting, playful journey through songs, games, stories, and dialogue. Kids explore both local and international languages in a way that builds confidence, sharpens communication skills, and nurtures curiosity From learning modern instruments to performing in live showcases, students experience a journey of joy, discipline, and deep language pride.

Fashion Show Program

Experience the world of fashion with runway shows, styling workshops, and design challenges. Participants will explore traditional Rwandan attire and modern fashion trends, learning about fabrics, colors, and sustainable design practices.

All youth aged 6–20 interested in learning and creative activities.
